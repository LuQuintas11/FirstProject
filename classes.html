Classes can be online or in person! Check below what you will learn

Basic Piano

The classes are individual and therefore are adapted to the level of the student. It is not necessary to have any prior knowledge. In addition to Piano, Keyboards or Electric Bass, musicians who technically dominate other instruments and need to learn or develop techniques can take classes.

Theory

Essential basic concepts to be able to read and write music.

  • Harmony: Modern tonal harmony and its operation and application in popular music. Jazz harmony, contemporary classical and modal harmony and its application in modern jazz.
  • Improvisation: Application of the harmonic and rhythmic concepts studied

Audioperceptive

Through a gradual process, you will acquire the ability to aurally recognize all musical elements: instruments, melodies, scales, intervals, harmony, and rhythms.oriented to develop the musical sense and at the same time to be able to understand from all the possible aspects the music that is listened to.

Composition

Expansion of the concepts of harmony and improvisation, analysis of works and styles and their application in their own music. Concepts and ideas for arrangements and instrumentation in different styles.

  • Reading: Development of the ability to read at sight through progressive exercises appropriate to the student's level that present the necessary difficulty to both maintain interest and be overcome without difficulty.
  • Writing: Apply reading knowledge to be able to write any musical idea
